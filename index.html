How to Put a your own font on Your Website: Papyrus

In this assignment, I am bringing an uncommon font, Papyrus, to my website.

Steps to Get Your Own Special Font:

  1. Pick a unique font like Papyrus for your website.
  2. Get the font files in different types, such as .woff, .woff2, and .ttf.
  3. Send those font files to your web server.
  4. Create a CSS file and make a rule called @font-face to tell the website about your font.
  5. Use that special font for the text on your website using CSS.
  6. Create a webpage explaining the about the process of serving your own font into your webpage.
  7. Upload your webpage and CSS files to your web server.
  8. Visit your webpage using the appropriate URL.

By following these steps, you can give your website a unique touch with a special font like Papyrus, making your content look different.
